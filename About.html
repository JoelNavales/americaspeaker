Our Mission & Story

Connecting patriots with the voices that matter.

Born Out of Necessity

It started with a simple problem: county chairs, event organizers, and local patriots were spending hours making phone calls, trying to find trusted voices who shared their values.

America First Speakers was built to solve that problem. We bridge the gap between the grassroots movement and the thought leaders who inspire them.

We believe that the right speech, at the right time, can spark a movement. We are dedicated to ensuring that conservative values are heard loud and clear in every town hall, convention center, and gathering place across the nation.

Our Core Values

Liberty

We champion individual freedom and the constitutional rights that make our nation unique in history.

Integrity

We vet every speaker to ensure they align with the values of the audiences they address. No surprises.

Patriotism

We are unapologetically proud of our country and dedicated to preserving its heritage for future generations.
